What's the Domain Tokens

You can control your own domain via a Token.

CryptoName a decentralized human-friendly solution to own your crypto currency addresses.

By using your own name, you can register your personal CryptoName address e.g. brian.elastos.name.

You will need only your brian.elastos.name to receive Elastos, Bitcoin and Ethereum.

Your CryptoName can be transferred to others.

Brian.elastos.name is your passport to the crypto world, you can link your CryptoName address to your wallet address, DID, email, website and so on.

The Elephant Wallet will support elastos.name. So, all what your friends need to do is to enter your name if they want to send you an encrypted token.

Features

You are the real owner

With proof of ownership based on the blockchain token, no one can take away ownership of your CryptoName.

Based on Smart Contracts

Based on the Elastos Ethereum Sidechain allow the users to buy or resale their CryptoNames.

Human friendly names

Once registered, there is no need to use the long confusing hash address, simply give the sender your CryptoName.

Anonymous

To purchase a CryptoName, you do not need to give any personal information. It is completely anonymous.

Transferable

Once you own your CryptoName, it is your asset. You are free to give and to sell to anyone that you choose.

Self-Sovereignty

You have undisputed authority over your CryptoName, which means it can't be controlled by others or proxied.
