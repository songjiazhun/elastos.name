What's the Domain Tokens

You can control your own domain via a Token.

You can register a Domain Token for your name, eg. ABC.elastos.name. ABC is your name.

You will receive an ABC Domain Token when you registered.

You can setup ABC.elastos.name page and only the people who holds ABC Domain Token can setup the ABC.elastos.name page.

You can transfer the ABC Domain Token to others.

The ABC.elastos.name page is your personal homepage in the crypto world. You can set up your wallet address, DID, email, website and so on.

We plan to ask Elephant Wallet to support elastos.name, if it is done, when your friend sends you an encrypted token, he will only enter your name.

Self-sovereignty

How to get a domain?

Only need a three steps you will get an own domain name.

-1-

Prepare a metamask wallet

You can create a new metamask or import your mnemonics to the metamask wallet. Then you must connect to elastos eth sidechain and save some ELA to your metamask.

-2-

Register your domain name

Open the Register Page to register your name. That needs you to pay the ELA.

-3-

Setup your domain

Fill the domain info and submit them, you can see them in one second.
